About us

Our website design company focuses on making the ideal website for both the business and the end user. We have created websites tailored to each client's needs. Our company provides this service to a wide range of companies, large and small, as well as for individuals. To create a site that helps customers to achieve their goals and help their businesses grow, our team works closely with customers. Sites that are high quality, affordable, and give users a memorable experience are what we strive for.

Hot Beans is an online web development enterprise that was founded in 2016 by Jaiden Carrington, when Jaiden Carrington was doing his research about London businesses, he was looking for ideas for what type of business should he open for the next big thing such as retail or fast food. But then he realise with that many businesses and attraction in London he could start a web development firm to help mutiple businesses upgrade and perfect their website to look user-friendly and attract the attention of potential customer.
